## The Cost Asymmetry Problem

| Iran's Weapon | Cost | US/Allied Interceptor | Cost | Ratio |
|---------------|------|----------------------|------|-------|
| Shahed-136 drone | $20,000 | Patriot PAC-3 | $4,000,000 | 1:200 |
| Shahed-136 drone | $20,000 | SM-6 | $4,300,000 | 1:215 |
| Shahed-136 drone | $20,000 | THAAD | $12,770,000 | 1:640 |

Iran produces 200-500 Shaheds/month. This is **economically unsustainable** for defenders.

## Single-Point Production Failures
- **Ammonium perchlorate** (rocket motor oxidizer): ONE facility. 96 hours consumed 6.7% of annual capacity
- **RDX/HMX** (high explosives): Holston Army Ammunition Plant, Tennessee — sole source
- **Rare earth magnets** (guidance): 80,000+ parts depend on Chinese-controlled minerals
