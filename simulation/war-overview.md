# War Overview & Timeline

## How It Started
On **February 28, 2026**, the United States and Israel launched a coordinated strike campaign against Iran — approximately **900 strikes in 12 hours** targeting:
- Military infrastructure and air defenses
- Missile production facilities
- Leadership targets
- Nuclear enrichment facilities

**Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei was killed** in the initial strikes, along with dozens of senior Iranian officials.

The war is designated **Operation "Roaring Lion"** by Israel and **Operation "Epic Fury"** by the US.
