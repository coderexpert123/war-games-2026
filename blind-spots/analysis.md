## What Remains Unmodeled

These are acknowledged limitations, not research gaps:

1. **Black swans**: Assassination, accidental nuclear incident, pandemic during war — inherently unpredictable
2. **Exact military operational outcomes**: Classified capabilities, fog of war
3. **Individual leader psychology**: Decision-making under extreme stress is not modelable from open sources
4. **Feedback loop nonlinearity**: Many cascades interact in circular ways that resist linear modeling
5. **Timing of tipping points**: We know the pressures but not exactly when systems break
